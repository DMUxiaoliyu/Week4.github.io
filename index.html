1. ** Opening Equipment: ** The size of the front angle of the tool angle determines the sharpness of the cutting edge, mainly by the hull material and tool material decision. The hull material is mostly 45# steel, medium strength, the tool material is cemented carbide, good bending strength and toughness, so you can choose a larger front angle, select the front angle Î³ = 20. The main role of the back angle is to reduce the friction between the tool back face and the machining surface in the cutting process, appropriate increase in the back angle can reduce tool wear, take the back angle of 10 degrees, edge tilt angle is generally between 0 and 5 degrees, take the edge tilt angle of 1 degree.
2. ** Robotic Arm End-effector** The force measuring module, normal vector detecting module, support unit and motorized spindle of all the structural parts of the end effector are relatively heavy and have good controllability.
3. **Analysis of motion planning of mechanical arm ** The flange of the drive motor and the rotating plate of the base are fixed by bolts. The rotation of the motor drives the rotating cover to rotate. Two angular contact ball bearings are installed on the bearing seat to share the load on the output shaft of the drive motor to prevent the output bearing from being too affected by the load.
